Individual Legal Documents

Prefer to start with a single document? Select one of the options below to begin your guided intake. You’ll answer a short series of questions, and E-LegalBoom will prepare a custom document framework for you.

Partnership Agreement

Defines ownership, profit sharing, decision-making, and responsibilities between two or more business partners.

LLC Operating Agreement

Sets the rules for members of an LLC – capital contributions, voting, management, and distributions.

Joint Venture Agreement

Temporary or project-specific partnership between two or more parties for a defined business opportunity.

Shareholder Agreement

Governs rights and obligations of shareholders in a corporation, including voting, transfers, and protections.

Employment Agreement

Sets out duties, compensation, confidentiality, and protections for employees of your business.

Independent Contractor Agreement

Defines the relationship with non-employee contractors, including scope, pay, IP, and liability protections.

Buy–Sell Agreement

Owner-to-owner agreement covering death, disability, exit, or sale, with clear valuation and buy-out terms.

Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

Protects confidential information when sharing ideas, proposals, or business details with others.

Website Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy tailored for small-business websites collecting basic customer or visitor data.

Website Terms of Service

Terms & Conditions for users of your website or online service, including disclaimers and limitations of liability.
